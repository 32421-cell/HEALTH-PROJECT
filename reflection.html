Personal Reflection

What’s one thing I’ve learned about digital wellbeing?

I’ve learned that cyberbullying affects mental health and that online behaviour has real consequences.

What’s one digital habit I’d like to improve?

Reduce late-night scrolling and avoid reading negative comment threads.

How could this change benefit my health and wellbeing?

Improved sleep, reduced anxiety, and better focus at school.
