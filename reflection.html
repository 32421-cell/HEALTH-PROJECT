Personal Reflection

What’s one thing I’ve learned?
I’ve learned that cyberbullying can affect people’s mental health just as strongly as in-person bullying.

What’s one digital habit I’d like to improve?
I want to stop scrolling through negative comment sections that bring me down.

How could this change help me?
It would help me stay positive, reduce stress, and focus on supportive online communities.
