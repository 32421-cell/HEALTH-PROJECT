Ask the Wellbeing Wiz!

Dear Wellbeing Wiz, I spend 5 hours on TikTok every night and feel exhausted. How can I cut back without missing out?

Start small: set a 30-minute bedtime screen curfew and use your phone's screen time controls. Replace late-night scrolling with a short walk, music or a hobby. Tell friends you’re reducing time — they'll support you instead of making you feel left out.

Dear Wellbeing Wiz, I keep getting mean comments on Instagram. Should I delete my account?

Not necessarily. Use block & restrict tools, turn off comments, and report abusive users. If the platform is harming you more than helping, taking a break is OK. Save screenshots and tell a trusted adult if comments are threatening.

Dear Wellbeing Wiz, My friend is being bullied in a group chat. What should I do?

Check in with them privately, offer support, and encourage blocking/reporting. If you're comfortable, call out the behaviour in the chat firmly (without escalating). If it's a safety issue, tell a teacher or guardian.
