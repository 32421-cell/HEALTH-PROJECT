Q1: I keep getting mean comments on Instagram. Should I delete my account?

A: No need to delete your account! Block the people, report the posts, and talk to a trusted adult. Bullies want a reaction â€” not giving them one is powerful.

Q2: My friend is getting bullied in a group chat. What should I do?

A: Privately message your friend, check on them, and if safe, speak up in the group. Encourage them to block and report the bully.

Q3: In online games, I get nasty messages. How can I stop this?

A: Use mute, block, and report tools. Play with friends when possible. Screenshot messages and tell a trusted adult if it continues.
