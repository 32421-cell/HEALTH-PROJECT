Why our site is important

Cyberbullying can follow someone into their most private spaces. Our goal is to explain what it is, provide real tips, and show resources where you can seek help. Use the menu above to explore each section.

Quick tips

  • Use block/mute tools — they work.
  • Keep evidence (screenshots) if someone harasses you.
  • Tell a trusted adult — you don’t have to handle it alone.
